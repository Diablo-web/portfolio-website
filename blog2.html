Top 5 underrated website that will make your life easier.

1. Class Central

Helps you to discover free online courses from top universities in the world. Provides best courses on almost any subject, wherever they exist. It will meet your requirement with all courses they provide.

Class Central

2. Interview Warmup

Finding the job on internet is very easy, but the journey to land on top job can be a big battle. Landing on interview is only like half battle won. So, the next step is to prepare for interview and it can come with anxiety. Thanks to Google, it is a website by google that helps you to prepare for job interview in IT Support, Project Management, UX Design, Data Analytics, E-commerce.

Interview Warmup

3. iLovePDF

Every tool you need to work with PDFs in one place. It helps you to edit all your PDFs in seconds. Convert, Split, Merge and many more. Every tool you need to use PDFs, at your fingertips. All are 100% FREE and easy to use! Merge, split, compress, convert, rotate, unlock and watermark PDFs with just a few clicks.

iLovePDF

4. Alternative.To

AlternativeTo is a internet site which lists alternatives to web-primarily based software, desktop or laptop software program, and cellular apps, and kinds the options by using various standards, it helps to find better alternatives to every software or app. Users can also search by tag to find software, which offers an alternative way of finding related software. Users can also narrow their search by focusing on particular platforms and license types (such as “free for non-commercial use”).

Alternative To

Pexels provides incredible image without paid subscription or in keeping with image use charges. It is a excellent platform for photographers and digital artists who are looking to build a portfolio and recognition. Here you can get any images and videos for any topics that you want.
